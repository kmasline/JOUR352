Navient Lawsuit Includes 129 Illinois Complaints

By Kurt Masline

Illinois had the most complaints each towards student loan service company Navient out of six states in the Midwest, data from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau shows. A total of 388 complaints were made in Illinois, Wisconsin, Indiana, Ohio, Missouri and Iowa since April 2019. The Land of Lincoln accounted for a third of the complaints during this period, with a total of 129. Ohio had the second highest number of complaints, with 105.
